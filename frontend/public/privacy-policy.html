Ghost Bazaar

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

Ghost Bazaar is a demo frontend for an agent-to-agent commerce experience. This Privacy Policy explains what information we may collect when you visit the Ghost Bazaar website at ghost-bazaar.vercel.app.

Information We Collect

We may collect limited technical information such as browser type, device information, page requests, and basic analytics needed to operate and improve the website. If you connect a wallet or interact with embedded third-party services, wallet addresses and related session metadata may be processed by those services.

How We Use Information

We use information to operate the website, maintain security, troubleshoot issues, understand demo usage, and evaluate product interest. We do not sell personal information. For wallet onboarding or payment-related experiences, third-party providers such as MoonPay may collect and process data under their own terms and privacy notices.

Third-Party Services

Ghost Bazaar may link to or embed third-party services, including hosting, analytics, wallet, and payment providers. These services may collect information directly from you. Their use of data is governed by their own privacy policies, not this one.

Data Retention

We retain limited operational data only for as long as reasonably necessary to run, secure, and improve the demo or comply with legal obligations.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information, but no internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed fully secure.

Your Choices

You may stop using the site at any time. You can also avoid connecting a wallet or using third-party onboarding flows if you do not want those providers to process your information.

Contact

For demo-related questions, contact the Ghost Bazaar project team through the project repository or official project profile linked from the website.
